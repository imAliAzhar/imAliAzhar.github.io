ABOUT

Hi! I'm Ali from National University of Sciences and Technology. I'm a third year Software Engineering undergraduate working as a research intern at NUST-TUKL Lab where my work is focused on Deep Learning models and applications. I have worked with YOLO, NVIDIA DIGITS, Facebook's Sharpmask along with a few other frameworks. Right now, I am working on recommendatory systems.
I also have a strong grip on front-end and back-end Web technologies including Bootstrap, JQuery, Drupal and Flask. I love making eye candy user interfaces. Sometimes I wish, I was a graphic designer.

I often write on Quora here.
